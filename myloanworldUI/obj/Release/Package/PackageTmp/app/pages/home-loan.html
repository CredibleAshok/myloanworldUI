A “home loan” is a loan to you by a bank or financial institution for the property you are using the loan to buy. Generally, a home loan will be a 25 or 30 year term, with regular repayment amounts fortnightly or monthly that are designed to pay off the loan over the contracted term. This is a kind of loan which is treated as secured and can be easily taken. The Banker may ultimately require you to sell the property to settle the debt. In India, This is a kind of loan taken most.
